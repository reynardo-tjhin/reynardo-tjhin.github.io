## A Bit About Myself

🎓 I studied at the University of Sydney from 2020 to 2024. I studied a Bachelor of Advanced Computing, majoring in Finance and Computer Science.

💰 I wanted to study Finance to understand how to accumulate wealth through investment in stocks. However, I realised that it requires a lot of work, experience and understanding of the markets to beat the market. Now, I just trade stocks without using the finance knowledge that I got from my university years. But, I do maintain my Excel design from time to time at work 😅. 

🤖 Moreover, I studied lots of Artificial Intelligence back in my uni days. But I realised I could not build and deploy software from scratch even after graduating from my Computer Science degree. So now I'm just learning how to build and deploy software to the real world. I also realised having a Masters degree is better than just a Bachelor degree to get a job in the AI field, and the job market is very unforgiving right now 😭. But I am tired of studying and taking exams 🫠. So, back to self studying and working 😎.

🎯 I don't really have a specific goal in my mind. I wish to be able to keep learning and keep improving myself.

🤩 I am currently interested in:

- Web Scraping
- Backend Development
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
